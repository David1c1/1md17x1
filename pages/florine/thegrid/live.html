Life Hacks

By Florine Hofmann, October,2017

10 Ways to Take A Break

Yes, yes, yes our schedules are full, our social calenders even fuller but sometimes it is important to turn out attention inward and take care of our well being. By taking care of ourselves in a wholesome way, we apply preventative measures that keep our bodies and minds from going on strike when it is most inconvenient.

 

Here are our top 10 tips for wholesome indulgences that feed the mind, the body and the soul.

 

Go for a walk

and explore nature. Put on comfortable clothes in which you can run and feel like a child and then go explore. Listen to music while you do it.

     

Take a bath

Try different oils, essential oils and herbs, throw in a bunch of flowers and make it smell like the most luxurious bath in the world. If you get bored while taking baths, a nice drink and a good book or movie are a good addition.

 

Declutter

There is no better feeling than simplifying and getting rid of the things we don't need because we make more space for the things we love.

Decluttering books we can highly recommend are: The Curated Closet and The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up

 

Create a piece of art

But without restrictions and a lot of color. Photograph, sing, sew, draw, write, practice a play.

 

Read a book

But not the book that you've been storing away for too long, the one you should read but never really felt like reading. Go to the library and stroll through your favorite sections, or find out what your favorite sections are. Find a book that really interests you in the moment and bring it to a place you always wanted to read in.

 

Write a letter

But not one of those fast impatient letters we are used to. Write one that comes from the heart. A letter that means more than the words in it.

   

Meet a friend

Preferably at their place or at a coffee shop so you'll have to go outside and look presentable and don't have the stress of having to entertain. Sometimes a good conversation is all we need to give us perspective.

 

Visit family

Being with my family sometimes helps to feel at ease, comfortable and taken care of and it is the perfect way to rejuvenate after a stressful time.

 

Go and see a movie

Bring your own snacks and make it a special day. We like to bring sushi in a reusable container and our favorite drink in a reusable bottle.

 

Force yourself

This might seem counterproductive but sometimes all we need is a kick in the butt to get started.

 

Sometimes even doing something good for our bodies seems like too much effort, which makes it necessary to occasionally force ourselves to do something good for ourselves.
