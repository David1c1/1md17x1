About

THE GRID

The word "the grid" is basically an abbreviation of the phase "grown kid", which we use as a term of indeerment for young adults and students trying to figure it all out.

 

Our student years are said to be some of the best years of our lives, yet, while we are in it, it might somethimes feel overwhelming and hard to navigate through.

We at "the grid" are dedicated to highlighting the perks, the fun and the joy of everyday student life, while acknowledging the struggle and giving useful life hacks along the way.

 

Our focus lies in giving you students the tools to create a valuable and holistic experience rather than "just" checking another thing off our CV-achievement list.

 

In short, the "grid" is a curated online space for students who are looking for study tips, budget friendly activities, quick-to-make recipes and general life improvement hacks.
