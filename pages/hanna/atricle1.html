Cheap doesn't
mean junk

If you usually think about cheap food, first thing that comes to your mind is probably FAST FOOD . Fast foods are serving not only cheap but also really really tasty food. As kids all of us felt excited when mom or dad agreed on buying a HAPPY MEAL. But still, one of the biggest problem with them is that they are not healthy. I mean you are not going to die after eating one cheeseburger but if you don’t have time it may become your daily routine.

Especially if you are a student.
Especially if you are an International Student.
Especially if you are an International Student in Denmark.

Aarhus - one of the biggest cities in Denmark. Capital of culture in 2017 and what is the most important - Denmark’s best education city. Which of course include students from all around the world.

As an international student every day is a struggle. The biggest insecurity is for sure lack of money. That’s pretty obvious. When next weeks fly by we all are getting used to it. But the real question is
Can you at the same time eat good and cheap food?

Sure, you can. Only thing you need is willingness. Put some effort into this. Not only treat food like something necessary to survive but in fact pretty nice part of the day. In Aarhus, on probably every street you can find a shop. Perfect place to do everyday groceries. Walking around the city you can see Fakta,Fotex, Lidl, Netto, Kvickly, Rema 1000 or one of the most popular in the whole wide world 7/11. Which one is the cheapest? Student as student always has got tactics. Each of them ( excluded 7/11) have got its goods. And by goods I mean budget food.

Fresh food doesn't have to be pricey, and this recipe is a proof.

Pasta with tomato sauce and meat!
Ingredients:

  • One Onion
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pasta!
  • Pepper, salt ( basil, oregano, chili optional if you like)
  • Oil
  • Cheese at the top

Preparation:

  1. 1.Boil water for pasta
  2. 2.At the same time at frying pan put a little oil
  3. 3.Chop onions and add it to the pan
  4. 4.Add meat
  5. 5.Boil pasta
  6. 6. When meat won’t be raw anymore, add tomato sauce
  7. 7. Add spices as you like
  8. 8. Stir from time to time and cook for 5 minutes
  9. 9.Put pasta on plate, add meat with sauce, sprinkle with cheese
  10. DONE!Enoy your spaghetti!
