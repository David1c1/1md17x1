Florine Hofmann "It’s just about putting a little more intention into your daily life."

October 4, 2017

When did all of this start?

I grew up in a very sustainable household. My mom has always been really adamant about healthy and organic food, so I’ve always eaten this way. But then I saw a video of Lauren Singer by Story Seekers, and that was really inspiring. She lives a Zero Waste lifestyle in New York. I saw that video, and that night I put out all of my plastic things. It was really really inspiring. That was three years ago now.

Do you think you had already been thinking about this before since this shift was so instant, or was it more of a revolution?

It really was a revolution. It never even crossed my mind that it was a possibility to live without waste, or with little waste.
