Top 4 second-hand shops for
students in Aarhus

Genbrugsland Bazarvest

Placed in Bazarvest (Aarhus V)and well known for it’s friendly staff plus it’s low prices. This store has a semi-large selection (Mainly kitchen, clothing and decorational items). Definitely worth a visit if you’re looking for hidden gems. For all the neccesary information -
Check out their website

- Connected to Bazarvest
- Low prices
- Sweet employees
- Semi-large selection

Danmission

Actually there’s two Danmissions in Aarhus, placed in northen and sourthen centrum. Not the biggest selections but prices are low and it feels like a going back into the 80’es. Much like Genbrugsland the staff is friendly. New wares come in each week, so usually you’re able to find neat stuff first during the week.Check out their website

- Cozy atmosphere
- Low prices
- Sweet employees
- Average slection

Loppehuset

Loppehuset is a large store in Viby. It stocks up on supplies everyday and the selection ranges from the smallest item to the biggest furniture. Most customers seem to go there for the furniture though. I highly recomment bringing a car (maybe even with a trailer) if you choose to go buy here.Check out their website

- New items everyday
- Low prices
- Large selection
- Lots of furniture

Genbrugs terminalen

Terminalen is an organized facility where many different people can have their second-hand stand. It’s known for having almost everything - however this also results in prices being a bit higher than the other in this list. As a plus it’s commen to hagle here and they’ve got their own café. Check out their website.

- Hagling friendly
- Normal prices
- Huge selection
- Cheap café
