STUDENT DISCOUNT ON STADIUM TICKETS

There are many different tickets and tribunes to choose from when planning on going to Aarhus Stadium. But wich ones should you choose?

Loyal fan tribune

If you consider yourself as one of the many loyal AGF-fans, standing in a crowd with your beer while singing out battle cries, you probably want to be where the action is at.

the C3 tribune is exactly where you'll find this, since it is considered the loyal fan tribune, and it's almost always completely booked.

Other tribunes

If you don't like standing up between shouting fans during the entire game, or if you just want quicker acces between your seat and the toilet or the bar, there are different types of tickets to choose from.

The B-tribune is even completely free for children under 14 years, in case you're bringing some of those too!
