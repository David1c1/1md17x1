CHEAP BREAKFAST BUFFETS BY THE RIVER

There are tons of awesome deals on breakfast buffets along Aarhus River, but which one should you choose?

Be aware of the time limits

The breakfeast buffets all have time limits, so if you want to avoid paying double or make sure you arrive in time before all of the food is gone, you need to be aware of them.

This means you can't take too long deciding which place to have your breakfast at, so make sure to check out the time limits in the list below before making breakfast plans with your friends and family!
