VISIT THE DEERPARK "DYREHAVEN"

If you don't have a fear for deer, you should definitely take a walk in "Dyrehaven" and experience the deer on extremely close hold! Or you could bring a picnic and enjoy it on one of the many table-benches around the park!

Remember the carrots and apples

Experiencing nature should be free, and so is the deerpark! But buying some carrots or apples to feed the deer with will definitely be worth the money!

You should only do this if you're not afraid of getting really close to them, since they'll be all around you as soon as they see what you brought!

Remember not to feed the deer pasta or bread, since it's bad for them.

Pay attention to the rules

Since the park is the home of the deer, you should always follow the rules of the park and be respectful.

You're not allowed to bring your dog, ride a bike, run og jog, throw trash or collect the chestnuts and acorns from the ground, since it's what the deer feed on, when people aren't bringing them food.

You are however allowed to touch the animals, and you're even allowed to ski and ride sleds in the park during winter.

Where to find the park?

Dyrehaven is located at:

Ørneredevej 6
