Ten interesting facts about handball

1. Players cannot hold the ball more than 3 seconds.

2. Players cannot move more than 3 steps by holding the ball.

3. After ice hockey, handball is the fastest game on earth.

4. Handball is not so popular outside of Europe, but it is the second largest sport in Europe.

5. Handball is a mix between three other games: basketball, football and water polo.

6. Players cannot go inside the goalkeepers’s field, but the goalkeeper can go all over the court.

7. One of the most attractive things about handball is that players can make as many mistakes as they want.

8. When we talk about the size and the softness of the ball, it differs for men, women and children’s handball games.

9. A Swedish handball player, Johan Pettersson, almost died while he bumped into the goalkeeper in 2002, lost a couple of his teeth and almost swallowed his tongue.

10. One of the golden rules in football is that the players are not allowed to touch the ball with their hands, except for the goalkeeper. In handball it is exactly the opposite: players are not allowed to touch the ball with their legs except for the goalkeeper.
